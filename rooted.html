The Rooted Experience

 

Rooted provides the opportunity to connect with God, the church community,

and your purpose in unexpected, life-changing ways.

 

Rooted is offered weekly immediately following the 10:30am service.

While each week is unique, Rooted is designed to be a 4-week conversation.

 

Think of Rooted as a personal journey. A journey with the goal of deepening

 your connection with God, His Church, and your purpose in the big, epic story

He is writing through human history.

 

ROOTED Week 1 – Welcome to Bethel

Answers the question, “Can I belong here?”

 •  Pastor Introduction

 •  Who is Greg Roedell

 •  Sharing Bethel’s Story, Plan, and Vision from Pastor Greg

 

ROOTED Week 2

Answers the Question: “How do I grow?”

 •  Great Commission

 •  Personal Relationship

 •  Connecting – House Party

 •  Share Story of How I Grow

 

ROOTED Week 3

Answers the Question: “How has God shaped me?”

 •  Who God Made You To Be

 •  Do you know there’s a place for you?

 

ROOTED Week 4

Answers the Question:  “How can I get planted?”

 •  Water Baptism.

 •  Planting in the House.

 •  Why Church membership

 

 

Our mission is to help people live life to the

 where people can connect with God, with others, and with opportunities to make a difference in our world.

 

We want to help you on your spiritual

 journey – a journey we are all making together!
