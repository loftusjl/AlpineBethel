WELCOME TO CHILDREN'S MINISTRIES!

 

Let the children come to me and do not stop them, because the Kingdom of Heaven belongs to such as these.      -Matthew 19:14

 

There is no more important job for us as followers of Jesus than to raise up our children with hearts full of love, honor, and respect for our Savior. Here at Bethel, we strive to educate them in God's Word and teach them of His personal love for them. God's hand is upon the children when they come together to put God's Word in their hearts and learn to pray and worship together. Our goal is to instill the basics of Christ's teachings in a way that is fun and stimulating so they will enjoy coming to church and

build a lasting relationship with the Lord.

 

SUNDAY & MID-WEEK SERVICES

Newborn to 5th Grade

We offer several classes for all different age groups, from newborns to 5th graders! Our classes focus on developing your child's personal relationship with Christ and nurturing their spiritual growth. Each class caters to a specific age group using stories, crafts, and skits that engage kids and are relevant to their lives at that time.

 

We encourage you to come and check out our classes! Hope to see you soon!
